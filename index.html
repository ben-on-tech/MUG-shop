Frequently Asked Questions

How will my Seer printing order be shipped?

We partner with reliable shipping carriers to ensure your custom printing order arrives safely and efficiently. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express options are available at checkout for faster delivery, usually within 2-3 business days.

What are the shipping costs for Seer printing?

Shipping costs vary depending on your location and the selected shipping method. We occasionally offer free shipping promotions, so keep an eye out for those!

Can I track my Seer printing order?

Absolutely! Once your SEER order is dispatched, you will receive an email containing your tracking number. You can use this to monitor the progress of your delivery in real-time by calling for our support team.

What if my Seer package is delayed or lost?

While rare, delays can occur. If your package is significantly delayed or appears lost, please contact our customer support team immediately with your order number. We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue and ensure you receive your order.
